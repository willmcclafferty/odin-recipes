Gorditas Picadas

Gorditas Picadas from beside UAZ in Jalpa

Ingredients

  • Mazeca
  • Water
  • Pork Fat
  • Frying Oil
  • Refried Beans
  • Queso Fresco
  • Pulled Pork

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Mazeca by combining with warm water
  2. Form balls with the dough and flatten by hand
  3. Cook on frying pan 2-3 minutes per side
  4. Fry in oil for 2 minutes each side
  5. Cut open gorditas half way
  6. Fill with your choice of beans, queso, and carne!
  7. Take a deep breath and ground yourself before your life changes
