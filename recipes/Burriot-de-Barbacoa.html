Burrito de Barbacoa

Burrito de barbacoa de Fonda Los Burritos

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Refried beans
  • Queso fresco
  • Cilantro
  • Diced onions
  • Guacamole

Recipe

  1. Warm the tortilla
  2. Heat the barbacoa and refried beans
  3. Put the barbacoa, refried beans, and queso in the center of the torilla
  4. Roll the torilla
  5. Add guacamole and chili if you want
  6. Smile before taking your first bite knowing it will be delicious!
