Tacos de Biztec

Tacos de biztec from Carlos' resturant

Ingredients

  • Maztec
  • Water
  • Queso fresco
  • Biztec con chili
  • Cilantro
  • Diced onions
  • Guacamole

Recipe

  1. Mix Maztec and warm water
  2. Make a small ball and press in tortilla press
  3. Fry on grill at medium heat for 1-2 minutes on both sides
  4. Heat the biztec con chili
  5. Put biztec on cooked torilla
  6. Add cilantro, diced onions, and guacamole
  7. Take a moment to be proud of what you cooked and then enjoy!
